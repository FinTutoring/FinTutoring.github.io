Your Path to CFA Level 1 Success

The CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) Level 1 represents the crucial first step toward obtaining one of the most prestigious certifications in finance. With a pass rate of 43-45% in 2024, preparation requires a methodical and rigorous approach.

This comprehensive guide will accompany you in your preparation, from understanding the exam structure to advanced strategies for maximizing your score. Based on the latest information from the CFA Institute and proven best practices.

Key Statistics 2024

  • Pass rate: 43-45%
  • Recommended study time: 300+ hours
  • Estimated minimum passing score: 68-69%
  • 10-year average: 41%

Understanding the CFA Level 1 Exam

Format

180 multiple-choice questions

Duration

2 sessions of 135 minutes
≈ 90 seconds/question

Format

Computer-based exam
At authorized test centers

Important Characteristics

Question Structure

  • • 3 answer choices (A, B, C)
  • • No penalty for incorrect answers
  • • Use of qualifiers: "most likely", "least likely"
  • • No "true/false" or "except" questions

Exam Time Management

Optimal Timing

  • • 90 seconds maximum per question
  • • 60 seconds for simple questions
  • • 120 seconds for complex calculations
  • • 15 minutes reserved for review

"Flag & Skip" Strategy

  • • Mark difficult questions
  • • Move quickly to next questions
  • • Return to marked questions
  • • Never leave blank answers
