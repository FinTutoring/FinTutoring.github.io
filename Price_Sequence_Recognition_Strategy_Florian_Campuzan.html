1 Executive Summary

Systematized discretionary strategy based on price sequence recognition developed by Florian Campuzan, Sciences Po Paris graduate, CFA charterholder and AMF-certified.

  • Investment universe: nano, micro, and mid-cap equities listed on NYSE, NASDAQ, and AMEX
  • Execution: exclusively Market-On-Close (MOC) to avoid intraday noise and ensure consistency
  • Average position: 1.5% of the dollar-capitalized volume at entry
  • Average holding period: 6 days
  • Philosophy: breakout + bottom-fishing with dynamic risk/reward adjustment
  • Goal: capture natural asymmetries of small-cap markets
